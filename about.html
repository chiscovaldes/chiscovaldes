About

Chisco Valdés is a filmmaker and photographer, born in Guatemala based in Spain. His interests revolve around issues of identity, religion, dualities, and daily contradictions. Using the image as a means of investigating, his photographic work uses the mixture of fiction and reality in different formats to explore ambiguities and contradictions in cultural documentation of daily life.

Graduated for BA in Communications in Spain, as a student, produced, co-wrote, shot the short films Verte and Eclipse selected in more than 30 festivals worldwide and won 9 awards. He has worked in Spain, UK, USA, and Guatemala in commercials, music videos, fiction, and documentaries in various roles, with proven skills in direction and cinematography.

Selected for the residence 20 Fotógrafos sponsored by NatGeo in 2019 and for the Iberoamerican Meeting of Emerging Filmmakers Ibermedia 2019. His photographic work has been exhibited, featured, and published in GuatePhoto, Festival Casa América, C41Mag, Broad Mag and Fotodemic.

He is currently in the development of his project Janydman, in which he documented his 9-month experience of pursuing the American Dream..
